Customize everything you see

Customization has been one of the key concepts we've planned from the beginning. In the case that you do not use one of our features, don't like how it looks or just don't want it on your new tab, you can remove it, with one click in settings. We also include color themes to make the experience even more customizable in order to make it exactly how you like it.
